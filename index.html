You're going to fall in love

In Iceland, you can see the contours of the mountains wherever you go, and the swell of the hills, and always beyond that is the horizon. And there’s this strange thing: you’re never sort of hidden; you always feel exposed in that landscape. But it makes it very beautiful as well.

Adventures and breathtaking views

Walk through the mountains of Iceland. And as you come to a new valley, as you come to a new landscape, you will have a certain view. If you stand still, the landscape doesn't necessarily tell you how big it is. It doesn't really tell you what you are looking at. The moment you start to move the mountain starts to move.

Explore

The most amazing thing about Iceland isn't the lava fields or the glaciers. It is the fact that there are 13,000 km of roads that will take you into wilderness.

Meet nature

Iceland is home to and has been shaped by the primary forces that have moulded the entire Earth: volcanic eruptions, earthquakes, and erosion.

Find your way

When you are in a place like Iceland, allow yourself to take a little more time to divert off onto other paths for a while and see what comes to you.

Fall in love

There's no time to be bored in a world as beautiful as this. In Iceland, you will reach your destination even though you travel slowly.
